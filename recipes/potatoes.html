Pan Fried Potatoes

Description

These pan fried potatoes are a great side dish or even main meal! I have lived off of potatoes before and let me tell you that these are some of the best potatoes you can make. They are super easy to make as well and are a great dish to serve at any party you may be having.

Ingredients

  • One pound of baby potatoes, scrubbed clean
  • One tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • One tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • One tablespoon of freshly chopped rosemary
  • Kosher salt or sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Bacon grease (optional)
  • Chili powder (optional

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes into coins about a quarter inch thick.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat oils.
  3. Add potatoes and season with rosemary, salt, pepper, and garlic
  4. Cook, undisturbed, until potatoes are golden and crusty underneath, 4, to 5 minutes.
  5. Flip potatoes and cook until golden on other sides about 4 to 5 minutes
  6. Add some more garlic powder or chili powder until potatoes are tender
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!
